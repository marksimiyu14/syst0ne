Access Control System
Tripod turnstiles

Tripod turnstiles are compact and cost-effective entrance solutions. Designed for smooth and silent operation, reducing power consumption, wear and tear. It is suitable for areas with a high traffic. By receiving a signal from the access control system, or pushing button, it allows the entry of one person in a time. Entry to different directions is electronically controlled. Default mode is to lock the mechanism until a valid authorization signal is received. In case of emergency, the horizontal arm will drop to allow free passage.

Full Height Turnstiles

Full Height turnstiles is the highest security kind of turnstile and designed in double door type for pendestrians access, which can make stricter force control. It has standard relay signal which can be intergrated with any kind of third party access cntrol system (e.g RFID , fingerprint and biometric reader device). Incase of emergency, turnstile gate will open automatically to allow free passage when power off.
